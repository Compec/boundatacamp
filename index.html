DataCamp'18

Boğaziçi DataCamp'19

You are invited to Boğaziçi DataCamp organized by Boğaziçi University IT Club Compec for the second time this year.

During the summit, with talks, you will recognize the business applications of data science with a technical approach and practice them in workshops.

Countdown to Summit

Date of summit: November 24

Wait for Registration

What

Boğaziçi DataCamp is a summit at which the field of data science with its business applications is discussed via talks and workshops are held where attenders can learn by practicing. It will be organized for the second time by Boğaziçi University IT Club Compec this year.

Place

Boğaziçi University
Albert Long Hall

Time

24 November

For Whom

Boğaziçi DataCamp is designed for you, if

  • You are a student from the departments like Computer Engineering, Management & Information Systems in which programing concepts and algorithmic approaches are covered,
    (if you are a professional, still welcome)
  • You desire to make use of "data science" in your projects and career path,
  • You are curious what "data science" implies with the related terms Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Image Recognition, etc.

17-18 Kasım'da Boğaziçi Üniversitesi Bilişim Kulübü Compec tarafından bu yıl ilk defa düzenlenecek olan Boğaziçi DataCamp'e sen de davetlisin.

İki gün boyunca konuşmalarla sektörü "Data Science" açısından teknik olarak tanıyacak ve workshoplarla doğrudan uygulayarak öğrenme fırsatı yakalayacaksın.

Get Your Ticket Free

What

Boğaziçi DataCamp is a two day summit at which the field of data science and its future is discussed and workshops are held where attenders can learn by practicing. It will be organized this year for the first time.

Place

Boğaziçi University
New Hall 105
BM B4 (For workshops)

Time

17-18 November

For Whom

Boğaziçi DataCamp is designed for you, if

  • You are a student from the departments like Computer Engineering, Management & Information Systems in which programing concepts and algorithmic approaches are covered,
    (if you are a professional, still welcome)
  • You desire to make use of "data science" in your projects and career path,
  • You are curious what "data science" implies with the related terms Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Image Recognition, etc.
